Discover Duluth & UMD

Duluth and the North Shore offer amazing views, trails, and lakeside stops. Here are a few highlights I love visiting, plus my reasons for choosing the University of Minnesota Duluth.

Why I Chose UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because I'm from Duluth and really like the culture, community, and natural beauty of this area. Growing up here, I've always appreciated the strong sense of community, the outdoor lifestyle, and the genuine friendliness of the people.

UMD offers excellent academic programs, particularly in business and finance, while maintaining that close-knit community feel I love. The campus is beautiful, the professors are accessible, and there's always something happening in Duluth. Plus, being able to study finance while staying connected to my hometown and family has been incredibly valuable.

My Favorite Duluth Spots

Canal Park

Walk the Lakewalk, watch ships under the Aerial Lift Bridge, and grab coffee. This is where I go to clear my head and enjoy Lake Superior's beauty.

Park Point

Long sandy beach for sunsets and relaxing by Lake Superior. My go-to spot in the summertime.

Campus Highlights & Personal Experiences

UMD's campus offers the perfect blend of academic excellence and natural beauty. I love studying in the library with views of Lake Superior, walking between classes surrounded by trees, and the sense of community that comes from being part of a smaller university within the larger University of Minnesota system.

Some of my favorite campus experiences include attending Bulldogs games, taking breaks by the campus pond, and milling around campus in general. The professors here are genuinely invested in student success, and the small class sizes allow for meaningful connections and personalized learning.
